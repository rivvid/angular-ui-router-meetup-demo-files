Angular UI-Router discussion

The Angular UI-Router service is available at https://github.com/angular-ui/ui-router

What is Angular UI-Router?

A service/module that replaces the default Angular router (ngRoute), enabling nested routes, or 'states'.

Unlike the $route service in Angular core, which is organized around URL routes, UI-Router is organized around states, which may optionally have routes, as well as other behavior, attached.

States are bound to named, nested and parallel views, allowing you to powerfully manage your application's interface.
